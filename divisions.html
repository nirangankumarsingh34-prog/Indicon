Division 01

CNS

Our Central Nervous System division develops precision-engineered therapies for neurological and psychiatric conditions that affect millions across India.

From anxiety and depression to nerve damage and sleep disorders, our CNS portfolio covers a comprehensive range of evidence-backed formulations — including our flagship Benfocon CD3 Tablet.

  • Neurological disorder management
  • Neuroprotective vitamin formulations
  • Nerve health & antioxidant support
  • Haemoglobin & brain function support

Division 02

Nephroscience

Our Nephroscience division is dedicated to renal health — providing solutions that support kidney function and improve quality of life for patients with kidney conditions.

Products include Neupro Renal, Neupro Albu and Carnotop-Plus — developed in collaboration with nephrologists to address an underserved need in Indian healthcare.

  • Renal nutrition powders (dialyzed & non-dialyzed)
  • Albumin support for hypoalbuminaemia
  • Cardiac & renal protection (L-Carnitine + CoQ10)
  • Electrolyte & protein management

Science-backed nutritional products designed to promote wellness, strengthen immunity and support optimal body function at every life stage.

  • High-calorie supplements (Neupro 2.0)
  • High-protein formulations (Neupro-HP)
  • Diabetic nutrition powders (Neupro-DM)
  • Micronutrient & immunity support
