About Us

Our Story

Dedicated to Advancing Healthcare

At Indicon Healthcare, we are dedicated to advancing healthcare through innovative pharmaceutical solutions. We have consistently strived to improve lives by developing and delivering high-quality medications that make a difference.

From the house of Indicon World, our pharmaceutical division operates with the singular goal of making quality healthcare accessible — driven by our belief that everyone deserves to Be Happy & Be Healthy.

Our Vision

To provide accessible and effective healthcare solutions that enhance the well-being of individuals and communities worldwide — committed to research, development, and production of pharmaceuticals that meet the highest standards of safety, efficacy, and quality.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Five principles guiding every decision, every product, and every interaction at Indicon Healthcare.

01

Quality

Unwavering standards in every aspect of our operations, from research and development to manufacturing and distribution. No compromise.

02

Innovation

Embracing pioneering technologies and continuously exploring new ways to address healthcare challenges. Always introducing the latest innovations in our products.

03

Integrity

Upholding the highest ethical standards in all our interactions and decisions. Transparency and honesty are the bedrock of how we operate.

04

Collaboration

Fostering partnerships with accomplished healthcare professionals, researchers, and organisations to drive meaningful advancements in medicine.

05

Patient-Centric

Prioritising the needs and well-being of patients in everything we do. They are our topmost priority — every formulation begins and ends with the patient.

Indicon World

From the House of Indicon World
